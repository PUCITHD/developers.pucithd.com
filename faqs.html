FAQs

General

Is PUCIT Help Desk recognised by PUCIT or PUCIT Administration?

No. PUCIT Help Desk is not recognised by PUCIT or PUCIT Administration. PUCIT Help Desk works independently under the supervision of PUCIT Alumni.

How does PUCIT Help Desk work?

PUCIT Help Desk is being managed by PUCIT Alumni with the help of students. Every year a new team take charge of management of PUCIT Help Desk, giving their precious time for this cause.

What is PUCIT Help Desk Developers Portal? New

PUCIT Help Desk Developers Portal is a guide to contribute to PUCIT Help Desk through code. PUCIT Help Desk host repositories on GitHub for benefit of students and applicants. One can simply fork repositories and create pull request.

What should a pull request have to be accepted?

A pull request should have a meaningful contribution on PUCIT Help Desk for students, applicants or alumni.
